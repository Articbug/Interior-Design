Spiral staircases These staircases feature a central post with radiating steps that spiral upwards to the floor above. They are substantial space savers, which makes them perfect for smaller homes. However, they can be challenging to navigate and make moving furniture a hassle. Curved staircases These staircases feature a sweeping design that follows a gentle curve or spiral, creating a dramatic focal point and adding a sense of elegance and fluidity to the space. They are popular in luxurious and contemporary settings, making a bold statement. U-shaped staircases These staircases are often utilized in spacious areas and can create a grand and impressive entrance. They are frequently found in luxurious residences, upscale hotels, and expansive commercial buildings. Metal and glass stairways These stunning stairs are a common sight in homes and businesses alike. Metal and glass stairs, whether they're conventionally straight or feature a unique central spine, provide a dramatic and sophisticated visual experience.
