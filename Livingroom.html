  1. Furniture: Choose a statement sofa, statement coffee table, and cushions to draw attention to the furniture.
  2. Consider timeless pieces of furniture that have an aristocratic or royal allure.
  3. Lighting: Incorporate different lighting options and ensure there's enough natural light.
  4. Floors: Decorate the floor with a luxury rug.
  5. Walls: Incorporate a big piece of wall art.
  6. Accessories: Add shiny elements like brass, gold, and silver. Consider luxurious fabrics such as faux fur, silk, and velvet.
  7. Windows: Ensure there are big windows, glass doors, or skylight roofs to let in natural light.
  8. >Overall feel: A luxurious living room should feel bright and open, even if the floor space isn't large.
